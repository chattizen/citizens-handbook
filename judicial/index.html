Tennessee's 11th judicial district covers Hamilton County and consists of four courts and two attorneys. Two courts of limited jurisdiction are also included here.

Chancery Court

The chancery court covers civil cases of an equitable nature.

Read more

Circuit Court

The circuit court covers a wide range of civil cases.

Read more

Criminal Court

The criminal court has original jurisdiction over criminal cases involving felonies and misdemeanors.

Read more

General Sessions Court

The general sessions court covers civil disputes and misdemeanors.

Read more

Juvenile Court

Juvenile court covers cases involving minors alleged to be delinquent, unruly, dependent or neglected.

Read more

District Attorney

The district attorney prosecutes criminal cases before the general sessions and criminal courts, and he prosecutes juvenile delinquency cases brought before juvenile court.

Read more

Public Defender

The public defender provides legal counsel to people who are accused of crimes and cannot afford a private attorney.
