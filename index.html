The Owners Manual provides an overview of the basic functions of local government in Chattanooga and Hamilton County.

City of Chattanooga

The city of Chattanooga is a municipal corporation that operates according to its City Charter and City Code. The government consists broadly of an elected mayor and nine-member legislative body called the City Council.

Read more

Hamilton County

Hamilton County is a political subdivision of the state. The government consists of many elected constitutional officers. The mayor and nine members of the County Commission are the most prominent ones.

Read more

Education

The Hamilton County Department of Education is a local education agency that executes state and federal policy. The department is governed by an elected nine-member Board of Education. Board members appoint a director of schools, or superintendent.

Read more

Judicial

Tennessee's 11th Judicial District is comprised of Hamilton County. It consists of more than a dozen elected judges, a district attorney and a public defender.
