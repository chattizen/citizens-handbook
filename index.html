What is Chattizen?

Chattizen helps you navigate local government. Find out how your tax dollars are spent with the tax receipt or learn how local government works with our guides to Chattanooga and Hamilton County.

The project is funded by the Benwood Foundation. To learn more, visit our about page or follow us on Twitter, Facebook or Tumblr.
